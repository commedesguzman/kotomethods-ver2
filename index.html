What is KOTO?

Koto Methods is a campaign aimed at improving the health ecosystem for people with diabetes. We also aim to inform the general public about health issues and create a deeper support system.

This campaign will focus on public education with an initiative to educate and inform the public to increase awareness about Diabetes. Our goal is to introduce accessible information to the public and create a support system that goes beyond the surface.

Due to various policies and regulations within the industry, there isn't much opportunity to communicate health information efficiently. Our goal is to open up the conversation about how we can view health in a more accessible way that considers communication, accessibility, and universal design.
